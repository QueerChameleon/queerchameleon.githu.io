Queer Chameleon is a project by creator Amee Wilson, started in the 2021 Covid-19 lockdown. The artist uses a wholesome comics about chameleons to talk about all things identity. Using different pride flags, the content is designed to give visibility to groups who may not usually find much content online about their experiences. Equal parts comedy and equal parts education, the comics are meant to be light-hearted and have so far reached over 10 million people.
